Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to DreamLife ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that might be collected through our goal-setting and task-tracking iOS application ("DreamLife" or the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Information We Don't Collect

DreamLife is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. All your goals, tasks, and roadmaps are stored locally on your device.

We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts
  • Monitor your usage
  • Store any data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Use analytics tools
  • Serve advertisements

Data Storage

All data you create within DreamLife, including your goals, tasks, and roadmaps, is stored exclusively on your device. This data remains private and accessible only to you through your device's local storage.

Children's Privacy

DreamLife is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, no special provisions are required for children's privacy under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) or similar regulations.

Security

While we do not collect or store any user data, we recommend that you:

  • Keep your device secure with a passcode
  • Regularly update the App to receive the latest security improvements
  • Back up your device according to Apple's recommendations

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.

App Store Data

When you download DreamLife from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect and share certain data with us as part of standard App Store analytics. For information about Apple's data collection practices, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at dreamlifemobile@gmail.com.
